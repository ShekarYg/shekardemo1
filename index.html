IMS Project Description................ The purpose of the project is to solve the business problem presented by a large number of process and replenishment tasks, taking up numerous store hours, and to help resolve stock availability issues. IMS (Inventory Management System) is a system lead replenishment tool that provides locations to excess inventory in a store and half-hourly picks to colleagues on the shop floor. The project will help to manage overstocks by providing locations to top stock and back of house locations. By using max shelf, PI and sales data, products can be replenished as soon as shop floor levels drop below a desired level of fill. Any errors are fixed at point of pick which improves availability and drives down the number of unnecessary overstocks.
